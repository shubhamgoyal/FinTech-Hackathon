Company Background
In this century, for the first time in human history, chronic diseases (Non-communicable diseases) kill more people than infectious diseases. That's a BIG DEAL. But it does not have to be. Holmusk is pioneering a new approach of looking at diabetes. We deliver the best of big data science, behavioral medicine and consumer technology to tackle chronic diseases.
Founders

Nawal Roy

Founder / CEO

Nawal is Founder and CEO of Holmusk - a global tele-health platform. Prior to this, Nawal was Co-Founder and CEO of HelloPay (Rocket internet venture). Before leading HelloPay, Nawal was Junior Partner leading client advisory service line in McKinsey & Company. He has been deeply involved with serving clients across North American, Europe, South East Asia, India, China, Middle East & Africa.

Shubham Goyal

Chief Technology Officer

Shubham is a hacker. He loves to build things and to find ways to get around obstacles. He was most recently involved at the Sensor Enhanced Social Media Centre (SeSaMe) at NUS. Prior to that, he has worked as a researcher at MIT, NUS and ICL. He loves attending hackathons and talking to others about random stuff. So, hit him up at shubham.goyal@holmusk.com

Yau Teng Yan

Dr Yau Teng Yan is a medical doctor in Singapore with 8 years of professional working experience. He completed his Basic Specialty Training in Diagnostic Radiology in 2014 while working at TTSH, SGH and NCC. He has clinical experience in various public hospitals as a medical officer, including in Emergency Medicine, Surgical ICU and Anesthesiology.
